About Vice City Web Edition

Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Web Edition brings the legendary open world experience of 1986 Miami directly into your browser. Set in a sprawling, neon-soaked metropolis inspired by 1980s Miami, Vice City stands as one of the most iconic environments in gaming history.

The story follows Tommy Vercetti, a mobster recently released from prison after serving fifteen years. He is sent to Vice City by his former boss Sonny Forelli to oversee a drug deal. When the deal is ambushed and both the money and the drugs are lost, Tommy is left to pick up the pieces.

As he works to recover what was lost and take revenge on the Forelli family, Tommy becomes involved with the city's various criminal organizations and power players. Over time, he builds his own network of businesses and influence, ultimately rising to control Vice City's criminal underworld.

This technical port is built on the reVCDOS codebase, which utilizes the reverse-engineered reVC engine. This version requires no downloads and no installation because assets stream dynamically as you play. It is a complete, self-contained experience that preserves every mission from the original 2002 release.

Key Features:

  • Integrated Save Manager: You can upload your existing .b save files from a PC or PS2 to resume your progress. You can also download backups to keep your virtual progress safe.
  • Instant Access: There are no large initial downloads. The WebAssembly engine loads in seconds and streams the map as you drive.
  • Cross-Platform Support: Play with a standard keyboard and mouse on desktop or use integrated touch controls on mobile devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the full game? Do I need to upload files? +

Yes, this is the full GTA Vice City experience. No file uploads are required. The game files (~701 MB) are downloaded automatically on your first visit and cached in your browser for instant loads forever after.

How does the Save Manager work? +

You can upload your existing .b save files from a PC or PS2 to resume your progress. You can also download backups to keep your virtual progress safe at any time.

Does it support controllers and mobile? +

Yes. Gamepads are fully supported on desktop browsers. On mobile, integrated on-screen touch controls appear automatically. Play in landscape mode for the best experience.

Why does the game show a black screen or run poorly? +

Ensure your browser supports WebAssembly and Service Workers (Chrome 110+, Firefox 111+, Safari 16.4+). Try a hard-refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R). On slow devices, set the FPS limit to 30 above.

Why are the physics acting weirdly or missions failing? +

The original game was designed for 30 FPS. Running at higher framerates can cause physics glitches. Set the FPS limit to 30 in the panel above to fix mission-critical physics.

My device is getting very hot. How do I fix this? +

Set the FPS limit to 30 to significantly reduce CPU and GPU load. This is especially recommended for mobile devices and low-end laptops to prevent overheating.
