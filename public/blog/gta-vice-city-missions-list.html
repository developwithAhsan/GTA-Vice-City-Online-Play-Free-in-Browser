GTA Vice City has 59 story missions spread across multiple employers and contacts. Unlike later GTA games, the mission structure isn't entirely linear — you often juggle missions from several employers at once. This guide lists every mission in the recommended completion order, so you always know what to do next.

A few missions are required to unlock others, so the order below follows the natural progression of the story rather than strict numeric order.

Ken Rosenberg Missions

Ken is Tommy's first contact in Vice City. These early missions establish the story and introduce you to the city.

Juan Cortez Missions

Colonel Cortez is sophisticated, diplomatic and occasionally very dangerous. His missions run parallel to the early story.

#MissionNotes

Working for Diaz is a pivotal part of the story — and eventually leads to a confrontation that changes everything.

Tommy's Own Missions (After the Mansion)

After the pivotal mansion raid, Tommy takes control. These missions unlock the second island and the endgame. They're the meat of the story.

#MissionNotes
17Death RowRescue Lance from the junkyard. Bring heavy weapons.
18Rub OutKill Diaz. This unlocks Tommy's mansion.
19ShakedownDestroy shopfronts in the mall.
20Bar BrawlEliminate protection from the Front Page Café.
21Cop LandGet 3 stars, steal cop car with Lance, plant bomb at mall. One of the hardest missions.
22Cap the CollectorKill the Forelli money collectors before they leave.
23Keep Your Friends Close...Final mission. Defend the mansion from Sonny's forces.

Beyond the main storyline, each property you buy comes with its own mission strand. Completing the Malibu Club, Pole Position, Boatyard, Print Works, Kaufman Cabs, Sunshine Autos, and Cherry Popper strands is required for 100% completion.

Hardest Missions & Tips

Demolition Man

Flying an RC helicopter to plant four bombs across a construction site under time pressure is notoriously annoying. The trick is to learn the map layout first — watch a short guide on the building structure, then fly a consistent route. Don't rush the early placements; speed comes from efficiency, not panic.

Cop Land

This mission requires you to intentionally get a 3-star wanted level and then steal a police car. The easiest method: grab a rocket launcher first (use the NUTTERSTOYS cheat or find one near the military base area), fire it once near a cop, then immediately jump in any police vehicle that arrives. Drive to the safehouse garage quickly.

Supply & Demand

You race by boat to a yacht to claim a drug cargo before other buyers. Fastest solution: have a fast boat pre-positioned at the dock. The Rio or the Squalo are both good choices. Take a straight line rather than following the waypoint exactly — the shortest path wins.
