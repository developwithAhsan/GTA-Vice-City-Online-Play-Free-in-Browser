GTA Vice City Rampages: All 35 Locations & Weapons

📅 June 2025 ⏱ 6 min read

Rampages are one of Vice City's most distinctive features — small skull icons hidden across the city that, when picked up, start a timed kill challenge. You have to eliminate a set number of enemies using a specific weapon before the timer runs out. All 35 must be completed for 100% completion.

Each rampage gives you unlimited ammo for the specified weapon during the challenge, so you don't need to worry about running out. When a rampage starts, enemies from a specific gang will spawn in the area around you. The key is knowing the weapon, knowing the area, and moving efficiently.

How Rampages Work

When you step on a skull icon, a rampage begins. On screen you'll see:

  • The weapon you must use
  • The number of kills required
  • A countdown timer (usually 2 minutes)

You keep the weapon after the rampage ends regardless of whether you pass or fail. Enemies will continue to spawn throughout the timer, so you never run out of targets — the challenge is moving fast enough to get the required kills in time.

Tips for Hard Rampages

Rocket Launcher rampages (#11, #26)

These require you to destroy vehicles, not kill people. Position yourself in an open area near the spawn points and fire at the vehicle doors as enemies climb in. One rocket per car is enough if you hit the body.

Chainsaw rampages (#9, #19, #34)

The chainsaw requires melee range, which is risky when enemies have guns. Move constantly — never stay still. Run toward a target, swing, step back, run to the next. Keep moving and you won't take much damage.

Minigun rampages (#20, #32)

The minigun has a short spin-up time — start holding the fire button a half-second before you aim at a target. With good positioning you can clear 30 kills easily in 2 minutes with this weapon.
